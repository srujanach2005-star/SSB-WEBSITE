Get To Know Us

Vision

At SSB TRAINING AND PLACEMENT PRIVATE LIMITED, our vision is to become a leading and trusted global partner in providing innovative software solutions, reliable manpower support, and impactful technology based training. We aim to empower organizations and individuals to achieve sustainable growth, operational excellence, and digital transformation. By bridging the gap between technology, talent, and training, we strive to be at the forefront of change driving progress across industries and creating a smarter, more capable workforce.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver scalable, high-quality, and customized software solutions, backed by skilled manpower and future-ready training programs. We are committed to equipping businesses with not only the right tools and people but also with the knowledge and skills required to thrive in an evolving digital landscape. Through hands-on training modules, real-world simulations, and continuous upskilling support, we empower teams to adopt new technologies with confidence. We believe in building long-term partnerships founded on trust, transparency, and results ensuring our clients receive end-to-end support that drives measurable success and growth.

Milestone

2025

Since our inception in 2024, SSB TRAINING AND PLACEMENT PRIVATE LIMITED has steadily evolved into a dynamic force in the fields of software development, manpower support, and technology based training. In the early months of 2024, we laid the foundation by building core teams and launching internship programs in Cybersecurity, Digital Marketing, and Technical Support. These initiatives marked our commitment to skill development and real-world training.

By mid-2024, we signed our first institutional MoU with Chalapathi Engineering College, Guntur, to foster academic-industry collaboration through workshops, internships, and hands-on sessions. Throughout the year, we conducted various technology-driven workshops across AndhraPradesh, focusing on Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, and emerging digital skills. In October, we delivered our first private Cybersecurity & Ethical Hacking Workshop, receiving overwhelming feedback for its practical and interactive approach. Towards the end of the year, we initiated cybercrime awareness programs targeting government school and junior college students demonstrating our social responsibility in promoting digital safety.

2026

In 2025, we expanded our technical capabilities and credentials by guiding interns and professionals through the Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) certification program. We launched our advanced online VAPT Internship Program with a hands-on learning model, further enriching our cybersecurity training ecosystem. To support deeper research and practical labs, we procured cutting-edge tools like Raspberry Pi 5, RTLSDR, and Alfa Adapters, enabling testing in wireless and automotive security domains. By mid-2025, we officially ventured into software and manpower support-based projects for private clients, offering services like online proctoring, biometric verification, and compliance monitoring. Additionally, we began building a full-scale technology training and deployment platform aimed at equipping students and organizations with future-ready skills to meet industry demands.
